Government Cybercrime Resources

What it is:

Government-operated services that receive, investigate, and respond to cybercrime complaints including online harassment, cyberstalking, impersonation, threats, and identity theft.

Official reporting links:

  • National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal
  • Cybercrime Helpline: 1930

Evidence needed:

  • Screenshots
  • Profile Links
  • Chat Records
  • Email Evidence
  • Incident Timeline

Reporting process:

  1. Visit cybercrime.gov.in
  2. Choose the complaint category
  3. Upload evidence
  4. Submit the complaint
  5. Save the complaint reference number
