Law Enforcement Support

What it is:

Law enforcement agencies investigate serious online incidents involving criminal activities such as threats, blackmail, stalking, extortion, identity theft, impersonation, sexual exploitation, or other violations of the law.

Official reporting links:

  • National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal
  • Emergency Police Helpline: 112
  • Visit your nearest police station

Evidence needed:

  • Screenshots of threats or abuse
  • Chat records and emails
  • Usernames and profile links
  • Phone numbers involved
  • Dates and times of incidents

Reporting process:

  • Collect and save all evidence.
  • File a complaint through cybercrime.gov.in or contact police.
  • Provide a detailed description of the incident.
  • Submit supporting evidence.
  • Cooperate with the investigation and keep the complaint reference number.
