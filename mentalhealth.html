Mental Health and Counseling Support

What it is:

Mental health and counseling services provide emotional support for individuals affected by online harassment, cyberbullying, threats, stalking, or other distressing online experiences. Professional counselors can help manage stress, anxiety, fear, and emotional trauma.

Official support links:

  • Tele-MANAS Mental Health Support
  • Mental Health Helpline: 14416
  • Alternate Helpline: 1-800-891-4416

Evidence needed:

  • No formal evidence is required.
  • Details about the incident may help counselors understand the situation.
  • Notes or screenshots can be shared if comfortable.

Support process:

  • Contact a mental health helpline or counselor.
  • Explain how the online incident has affected you emotionally.
  • Discuss coping strategies and support options.
  • Follow recommended counseling or mental wellness guidance.
  • Seek ongoing support if distress continues.
