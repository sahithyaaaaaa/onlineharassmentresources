Platform-Based Reporting Mechanisms

What it is:

Reporting systems provided by social media, messaging, gaming, and online platforms that allow users to report harassment, abusive content, impersonation, threats, or policy violations directly to the platform.

Official reporting links:

  • Instagram Help Center
  • Facebook Help Center
  • X Safety Center
  • YouTube Safety Center

Evidence needed:

  • Screenshots of the harassment
  • Usernames or profile URLs
  • Message logs or comments
  • Dates and times of incidents

Reporting process:

  1. Open the offending content or account.
  2. Select the Report option.
  3. Choose the appropriate violation category.
  4. Upload evidence if requested.
  5. Submit the report and block the offender.
