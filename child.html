Child Protection Services

What it is:

Child protection services provide support, safety, intervention, and reporting options for individuals under 18 who experience online harassment,image or video based abuse, cyberbullying, exploitation, grooming, or any form of online abuse.

Official reporting links:

  • CHILDLINE 1098
  • National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal
  • Emergency Child Helpline: 1098

Evidence needed:

  • Screenshots of messages or posts
  • Usernames or profile links
  • Chat records
  • Dates and times of incidents
  • Details of the platform involved

Reporting process:

  1. Contact CHILDLINE at 1098 or visit childlineindia.org.in.
  2. Explain the situation and provide available evidence.
  3. Report the content on the platform.
  4. File a cybercrime complaint if necessary.
  5. Follow guidance provided by child protection authorities.
