Digital Safety and Privacy Tools

What it is:

Digital safety and privacy tools help users protect their accounts, personal information, and online activities. These tools reduce the risk of hacking, impersonation, unauthorized access, and future online harassment.

Official resources:

  • Google Security Checkup
  • Meta Privacy Center
  • Microsoft Security Support
  • Apple Security Support

Evidence needed:

  • Security alerts
  • Suspicious login notifications
  • Password reset emails
  • Account activity records
  • Unauthorized access details

Protection process:

  • Review account security settings.
  • Change passwords immediately.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
  • Update privacy settings.
  • Remove unauthorized devices or sessions.
  • Monitor account activity regularly.
